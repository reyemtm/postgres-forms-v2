COZ Sanitary Line Cleaning App

The dark green lines are the active area for cleaning. The lighter colored lines are outside the active area. Any line can be marked as cleaned, even lines outside this active area.

To mark a line as cleaned, simply click on the line and the tap the Mark as Cleaned button. Once a line has been marked as cleaned, it cannot be marked as cleaned again or undone. If you need to unmark a line, click on it, copy the Pipe ID and send that to the email below. Once an area has been completed contact GIS.

This application is developed and maintained by the City of Zanesville. If you have any questions or have issues with the app please email GIS or call 740-617-4876.
